Bio

Shieldbearer

Shieldbearer is a bold new voice in Christian metal, forging anthems of faith, battle, and unbreakable hope. Blending powerful theology with raw emotion and cinematic storytelling, Shieldbearer's music carries the spirit of warriors who fight not with swords, but with truth. Every song is a call to stand, to believe, and to lift high the banner of Christ in a world at war.

Behind the name is Moncy Abraham, a solo artist and composer based in the USA. Shieldbearer is a solo project built from the ground up with a clear conviction: the name of Jesus is spoken plainly, Scripture is quoted directly, and faith is not decoration. It is the foundation every riff is built on.

"Christ is not hidden in metaphor here. He is spoken of plainly in the songs and in the conversation behind them."

Eternal Flames UK

The approach is intentionally hybrid: real guitars, real bass, real amps combined with AI-assisted production and cinematic sound design. No shortcuts on the theology. No apologies for the method. The result ranges from crushing metal anthems to ambient worship, all built with fire and purpose.

Moncy plays small solo gigs regularly. For booking, collaboration, or press, reach out directly at shieldbearerusa@gmail.com

How It’s Made

The Approach

Shieldbearer operates outside the traditional band model. Every element is intentional, from the production method to the theological content.

  • Real guitars — tracked through real amps. No modelling shortcuts on the core tone.
  • Real bass — played and recorded live to give the low end real weight and movement.
  • AI-assisted production — cinematic sound design, arrangement, and mix elements built with modern tools without apology.
  • Self-produced — every mix and decision made by Moncy. Full creative and theological control.
  • Scripture-first writing — lyrics are written from the text outward, not the emotion inward.
  • Solo live performance — small, intimate gigs. No full band touring setup required.
