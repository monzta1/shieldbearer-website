Bio

Shieldbearer

Shieldbearer is not the beginning of Moncy Abraham's musical story. It is the current expression of a calling and career that reaches back to 2000 across Christian rock and metal, live performance, songwriting, and ministry-minded music with an international footprint.

Behind the name is Moncy Abraham, a solo artist, composer, guitarist, lyricist, and audio engineer based in the USA. Before Shieldbearer, Moncy played lead guitar for WhitenoiZ and Scarlet Robe. Scarlet Robe opened for John Schlitt during his India tour in Bangalore, and Moncy also spent years playing concerts across Dubai and the UAE. Alongside the stage work, Moncy has served on worship teams across the USA, India, and the UAE, bringing the same theological conviction to congregational worship that now drives every Shieldbearer release. Shieldbearer carries that lived experience forward into a solo project built with a clear conviction: the name of Jesus is spoken plainly, Scripture is quoted directly, and faith is not decoration. It is the foundation every riff is built on.

"Christ is not hidden in metaphor here. He is spoken of plainly in the songs and in the conversation behind them."

Eternal Flames UK

Moncy is also the primary engine behind the work. He writes the lyrics, tracks and records guitars, builds the signal flow, mixes, masters, shapes the sonic detail, and brings a disciplined metal ear to every release. The approach is intentionally hybrid: real guitars, real bass, real amps combined with AI-assisted production and cinematic sound design. No shortcuts on the theology. No apologies for the method.

Beyond the audio, Moncy also handles the artwork, website buildout, social media presence, release deployment, and distribution. Shieldbearer is not a hobby project dressed up as a brand. It is the product of sustained work, technical skill, and decades of musical formation. The result is music forged by stage experience, cross-cultural performance, and theological conviction. For booking, collaboration, or press, reach out directly at shieldbearerusa@gmail.com

How It’s Made

The Approach

Shieldbearer operates outside the traditional band model. Every element is intentional, from the production method to the theological content.

  • Real guitars — tracked through real amps. No modelling shortcuts on the core tone.
  • Real bass — played and recorded live to give the low end real weight and movement.
  • Audio engineering — guitars are tracked with intent, signal flow is built carefully, and every mix and master is shaped by Moncy’s ear.
  • AI-assisted production — cinematic sound design, arrangement, and mix elements built with modern tools without apology.
  • Self-produced — the lyrics, production decisions, visual direction, and release execution stay under Moncy’s hands from start to finish.
  • Scripture-first writing — lyrics are written from the text outward, not the emotion inward.
  • Solo live performance — small, intimate gigs. No full band touring setup required.
