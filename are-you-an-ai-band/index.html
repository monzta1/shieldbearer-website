Are You An AI Band?

The title is a dare. Answer honestly about how you make music or how you listen to it, and find out where you land by the same standard the gatekeepers use.

Ten questions. Scored zero to ten. There are two paths because the tools reach you whether you build the music or only love it. Pick the one that fits, then be honest. Honesty is the entire mechanic here.

Do you make music, or do you listen?

Results are saved anonymously, meaning the path you chose, your answers, your score, and your browser type, so we can publish aggregate findings. No personal info is collected unless you add an email below to hear when the findings post.
