Short Bio — For Press Use

Who Is Shieldbearer

Shieldbearer is a Christian metal solo project by Moncy Abraham, based in the USA, and the latest chapter in a music journey that began in 2000. The project forges anthems of faith, battle, and conviction built on Scripture, not sentiment.

Every lyric names Christ plainly. Every song begins with a specific biblical text and works outward into sound. The catalog spans crushing metal, cinematic worship, and Scripture-only vocal arrangements — all made without apology, without ambiguity, and without a label gatekeeper.

Moncy is the project’s main lyricist and working audio engineer, handling guitar tracking, recording decisions, signal flow, mixing, mastering, and the sonic finishing work that gives Shieldbearer its weight and clarity.

Before launching Shieldbearer, Moncy played lead guitar for WhitenoiZ and Scarlet Robe. Scarlet Robe opened for John Schlitt on his India tour in Bangalore, and Moncy also played extensively across Dubai and the UAE. Shieldbearer has received coverage from Eternal Flames UK and Heaven’s Metal Magazine and is available for press, interviews, and booking.

Extended Bio

Full Artist Statement

Shieldbearer is not a debut artist story. It is the present form of a career built over decades in Christian music, live performance, and guitar-driven songwriting. Moncy Abraham has been active in the Christian music scene since 2000, carrying that long formation into a project centered on Christ, Scripture, and sonic force.

Before Shieldbearer, Moncy played lead guitar for WhitenoiZ and Scarlet Robe. Scarlet Robe opened for John Schlitt in Bangalore during his India tour, and Moncy’s performance history also includes significant concert work across Dubai and the UAE. That history gives Shieldbearer both an international perspective and a seasoned musical backbone.

Behind the name is Moncy Abraham, a solo artist, composer, lyricist, guitarist, and audio engineer based in the USA. The approach is direct: real guitars, real bass, real amps — combined with AI-assisted production and cinematic sound design. No shortcuts on the theology. No apologies for the method.

Christ is not hidden in metaphor in any Shieldbearer release. He is spoken of plainly. Scripture is quoted directly. Faith is the foundation every riff is built on. Shieldbearer releases drop directly to all major platforms — no label, no filter, no strategic softening for commercial reach.

The work also extends far beyond the songs themselves. Moncy handles the artwork, website buildout, social media presence, rollout planning, deployment, and distribution. Shieldbearer is a hands-on independent operation built by someone with both creative vision and the work ethic to carry it from lyric sheet to finished release.

The Production Approach

Shieldbearer uses a hybrid production model: real guitar and bass, tracked and committed, combined with AI-assisted orchestration, drum programming, mixing, and mastering. The engineering side is handled in-house by Moncy, from guitar capture and signal flow to final mix choices and master delivery. This is not a shortcut — it is a structural shift that gives a solo artist full creative and theological control without requiring a label or a production team. Full transparency on this approach is maintained across the artist’s website and all press conversations. See the Process page for a full breakdown.
