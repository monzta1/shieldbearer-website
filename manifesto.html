Declaration

The Shieldbearer Manifesto

This is a public statement about truth, censorship, and the freedom to read, test, and proclaim.

We are not inventing doctrine and we are not chasing controversy for attention. We are naming what has always been true and defending the freedom to examine it openly.

This Is Not New

The core claims of Scripture are ancient, public, and consistent. Shieldbearer is not introducing a new revelation. We are returning to what has already been written and proclaimed for generations.

The Industry Hid This

Gatekeepers treated theological clarity as a commercial risk. They rewarded ambiguity and pushed conviction to the margins. That was never neutral. It trained audiences to accept fog where truth should be plain.

Now You Have Access

Distribution is no longer controlled by a handful of executives. Artists can publish directly and listeners can decide directly. The wall between source and audience is breaking, and that changes everything.

Censorship Is The Real Issue

Censorship is not only deletion. It is also suppression, algorithmic burial, selective amplification, and soft pressure to self-censor. When people cannot test claims in public, power replaces truth.

Scripture Affirms Freedom

Scripture calls people to discern, test, reason, and choose. Faith is not coerced. Truth does not fear examination. A biblical posture requires open witness and honest testing, not protected narratives.

This Is About Freedom

This is about the freedom to hear the whole case, compare claims, and respond before God with a clear conscience. We reject propaganda from every side. We defend open proclamation and open accountability.

Our Stance

Shieldbearer will keep speaking Christ plainly, quoting Scripture directly, and refusing strategic ambiguity. We will not trade clarity for acceptance. We will not mute truth for reach. We stand for conviction, transparency, and the freedom to test all things in the light.

1 Thessalonians 5:21

Test all things. Hold fast what is good.

Shieldbearer is Christian metal with Scripture at the center. Christ is named plainly in every release and every conversation.
