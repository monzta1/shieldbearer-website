Silence in the Face of Evil

247 Views

Dietrich Bonhoeffer stood against Hitler's regime and refused to stay quiet while millions suffered. He helped form the Confessing Church, defied the Nazi-controlled pulpit, and was executed at 39, just weeks before the war ended. His words drive this song: "Silence in the face of evil is itself evil." A metal tribute to those who speak when the crowd goes quiet.
